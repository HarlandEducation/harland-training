📝 John Locke Competition
Announcement
An internationally recognised essay competition for secondary students. Develops critical thinking, independent research, and academic writing skills.
Particularly valuable for students preparing for university applications.
👉 Know a great fit? Reply in your Teacher LINE group with the student's name + a brief reason.
