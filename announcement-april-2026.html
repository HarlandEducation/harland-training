New initiative
🎯
Harland Academic
Coaching (HAC)
HAC is a different way to think about learning. Rather than following a fixed curriculum, sessions are shaped around each student’s specific gaps and longer-term goals — whether that’s a school project, an upcoming competition, or preparing for AP exams.
It works best for self-motivated students who are ready to take ownership of their growth. Lessons are more fluid, exploratory, and tied to what actually matters to them right now.
💡Ideal for goal-oriented, independent learners
📄Connected to real projects, exams & competitions
📈Already showing stronger student engagement
Have a student who might benefit? Speak to Admin to arrange training — or message the LINE group for more details.
